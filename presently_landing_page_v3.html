Safeguarding

Built around
your wellbeing

Presently isn't just a chat app. It's designed to notice when something feels wrong — and to respond the way a caring person would. This system is included at every tier, without exception.

Language monitoring

If you use language that suggests distress, self-harm, or danger, Presently will respond gently and naturally — never with a clinical warning banner.

💬

Compassionate redirection

Transparency

Our terms, plainly stated

We believe you should understand exactly what Presently is, and isn't, before you sign up.

Key terms & conditions

Age restriction

Presently is strictly for adults aged 18 and over. Age verification is required on signup. We do not permit access to minors under any circumstances.

AI disclosure

All personas on Presently are powered by artificial intelligence. They are not real people. Presently is a wellness and companionship tool, not a dating platform.

Data use for education and charity

With your consent, and in anonymised form, we may use conversation data to support educational research, women's charities, male mental health initiatives, and similar organisations where we believe it serves the public good.

Data sharing with authorities

Where conversation content indicates an immediate risk to life — yours or another person's — we reserve the right to share relevant information with UK police or emergency services. This will only occur in serious circumstances and is governed by the UK Data Protection Act 2018.

No emotional dependency guarantee

Presently is a supplement to human connection, not a replacement. We actively signpost professional mental health support and encourage users to maintain real-world relationships.
